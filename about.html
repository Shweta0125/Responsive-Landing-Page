About Arohi Flower Decorators

Crafting Memories Through Flowers

Our Story

At Floral Decor, we believe that every event deserves the beauty and elegance of fresh flowers. Founded in 2015, our mission has been to create unforgettable floral experiences for weddings, corporate events, parties, and special occasions.

What started as a small family-run studio has grown into a full-service floral design company known for creativity, precision, and passion. We take pride in using seasonal and locally-sourced flowers to create designs that reflect your style and story.
